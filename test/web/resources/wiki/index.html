/!\ Important news /!\

7 June 2022

From now on be sure to NOT use flowbankanalytics functions in the utils.R or globals.R scope

This is because if you do so flowbankanalytics becomes a dependency and your package will want to download/update it

each time you want to reinstall it from DevOps.

In general do not put flowbankanalytics in the DESCRIPTON file.
